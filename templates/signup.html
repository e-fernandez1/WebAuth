What Happens When You Submit
  1. Server re-validates every password rule (defense in depth).
  2. Password is salted and hashed with bcrypt at cost factor 12 — the plaintext is never stored.
  3. You'll be redirected to the login page.
  4. At login, after your password verifies, we email you a 6-digit one-time code.
  5. This signup event is logged to the public analytics dashboard.
